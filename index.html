Who is Exile

Born from a small city in the Netherlands, Amano Rosuko started producing and releasing tracks from his bedroom in 2020 on youtube and soundcloud. With a jazz background he started with some covers inspired by artists like Potsu, Atwood and rappers like DOOM and Nas until in 2023 he started releasing Breakcore, Jungle and drum and bass songs inspired by the likes of Sewerslvt, Goreshit, Nedaj and many more.

Indie artist

Unlabeled and striving. Exile Releases all his tracks on his own trough a distributor called TuneCore.

DAWs

Though starting off with the free DAW Reaper, Exile has openly prefered Ableton Live as his main DAW because of its user friendliness.

Art
